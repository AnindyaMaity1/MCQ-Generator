About

What is SathiNOVA?

SathiNOVA is an innovative platform designed to assist users in generating multiple-choice questions (MCQs) from various types of documents. It leverages the power of LangChain, Groq, and Flask to deliver an intuitive, user-friendly experience.

How does SathiNOVA work?

The platform allows users to upload PDFs, DOCX, or TXT files, from which it extracts key information and generates MCQs. Users can specify the number of questions, making it a versatile tool for educators, students, and quiz creators.
